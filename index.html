Automated Foley System in Computer Animation

Developed a real-time sound synthesis framework in Unity that generates material- and geometry-specific impact sounds through modal resonance models. Integrated C# scripts with Unity’s physics pipeline, enabling designers to add realistic audio by simply attaching a component. Experiments confirmed perceptual realism across shapes, materials, and collision forces.

Serverless vs Serverful Benchmark for imgproxy

Benchmarked a containerised image-processing service on Google Cloud Run and Compute Engine VMs to reveal trade-offs between cold-start latency and autoscaling throughput. Instrumented CPU logs and controlled experiments showed elasticity and container spin-up time as key drivers of performance.

Amazon Product Recommendation at Scale

Cleaned and analysed 14 GB of Amazon reviews with PySpark, engineered behavioural features and built collaborative-filtering models for personalised recommendations. Deployed an interactive Plotly Dash dashboard for stakeholders to explore product trends and sentiment.

BRCA Multi-Omics Prognostic Modelling

Explored TCGA breast-cancer multi-omics data and applied Lasso, SVM and feature-selection pipelines to predict hormone-receptor status with AUC 0.9+. Visualised shrinkage paths and ROC curves, highlighting biologically meaningful biomarkers.
